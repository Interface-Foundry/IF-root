Product Description

You can dress up and be comfortable too. This is a non-binding extra wide, comfort fit, mid-calf sock, for men and women with wider feet who are tired of squeezing into tight dress socks. These comfort fit extra wide dress socks are great for wide feet and swollen legs. Thinner dress sock weight. Fits well over casts. Great for people with edema or any condition where feet, ankles, or calves are wide or swollen. Made in the USA.
