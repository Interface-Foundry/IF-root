    By Swifty Lazar on November 10, 2014
    Size: MediumColor: Light Steel Verified Purchase
    Over the past two years I've noticed one thing about Hanes....they have absolutely horrible quality control when it comes to sizing t-shirts. Be warned, that no matter where you purchase these t-shirts, you are going to have issues, and it makes no difference what colors you buy. You WILL have issues with sizing.

    Buy a package of these shirts, and you'll notice that ALL of the lengths are different, and I don't mean my a marginal amount. I'm talking about a good 1"-2" difference in overall length between the longest and the shortest shirts. I hate t-shirts when they're slightly too short, and you're going to get at least one of those in these packs.

    Could I buy a larger size? Sure, but it's the exact same issue, and now I have a couple shirts that are too baggy just trying to find one that fits well.

    I recently purchased another package of these from a local store, and it's the same issue. I'm just going to have to stop buying Hanes t-shirts, and for years it was my "go-to" t-shirt to wear under sweaters, hoodies, etc., etc.
