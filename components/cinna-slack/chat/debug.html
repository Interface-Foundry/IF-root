    3.0 out of 5 starsSmaller Than I Anticipated
    By Mew on April 26, 2013
    Size: LargeColor: Black Verified Purchase
    I'm a 5'10" and 100lb female, but I typically buy plain/unisex-looking shirts/jackets because that's what I like. I purchased this in a men's large with the expectation of it being very large on me. I was, after all, looking for a large, comfy, sweat jacket that I could just throw on. This however, was smaller than I thought it would be. Have you ever seen a shirt/jacket that looked too wide in comparison to how long it is? Well that's what I thought when I saw this. It is a wide jacket with lots of extra room or 'puff' in the chest and stomach region. But in terms of length, it came to about my hips. And as far as the sleeves go, they're a tad too short. They fit okay when my arms are relaxed, but if I stretch or reach out for something, they ride up my arm.

    Pros: The inside of the jacket is very soft.
    Pros: The zipper works well.
    Pros: It feels really well made, given I've gotten some Grade-A Crappy Hanes sweatshirts before.

    Cons: Smaller than anticipated. If you're around my height or taller, and you want a loose-fitting jacket, I would certainly recommend sizing up. Maybe even getting it two sizes bigger than you usually go for. I'm the tiniest little thing, but I'm really tall, and I probably would have ordered a X-L or XX-L had I known how it fits.
    Cons: The draw string in the hood isn't the greatest. Sometimes sweatshirts come with material draw strings that are sewn at the end, others come with the cheat looking 'braided' drawstrings with the plastic tip at the end. And that is what this is. I think I'll just pull out the draw string all together, or replace it with one from an old jacket.

    All in all, good product for the price. I'll definitely wear it. I just wish I would have been more familiar with the sizing.
